Specify a URL to a BPMN 2.0 process definition file. You have many possibilities how to provide your business process definitions.
  • You can use the Drools Guvnor for the designing, storing and versioning your business processes. Your process definitions are accessible through the WebDAV interface:
    http://localhost:8080/drools-guvnor/org.drools.guvnor.Guvnor/webdav/packages/org.jenkinsci.plugins.jbpm/process.bpmn2
    Guvnor login credentials for jBPM plugin can be specified in the Jenkins global configuration page.
  • You can use any other valid URL location.
